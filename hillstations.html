Verdant Vistas

Velliangiri Mountains

The Velliangiri Mountains a part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve are situated at the Western Ghats of Coimbatore district, Tamil Nadu. Known as the "Sapthagiri, 7 Hills (Tamil: சப்தகிரி, ஏழுமலை) - Seven Mountains", these mountains are held and revered on par with spiritually most powerful place on the planet - Mount Kailash, the legendary abode of Shiva. On the top of the Velliangiri Mountains, Shiva is worshipped as Swayambhu, one who is self-created and in this form, he graces the devotees.

Airport : 1 hr 46 min (50km)

Railway station: 1 hr 18 min (38km)

Valparai

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Valparai, a serene hill station nestled in the lap of the Anamalai Range. Surrounded by lush tea estates, Valparai offers a picturesque escape with its mist-covered hills and cascading waterfalls. This hidden gem in Tamil Nadu, India, is a haven for nature enthusiasts and wildlife admirers alike. Explore the vibrant biodiversity as you encounter exotic flora and fauna, including the elusive lion-tailed macaques and vibrant birdlife. Valparai, with its tranquil landscapes and rich biodiversity, promises an unforgettable retreat, inviting you to unwind in the midst of nature's splendor.

Airport : 3 hr 24 min (120km)

Railway station: 3 hr 3 min (105.5km)

Topslip Tiger Forest

Immerse yourself in the enchanting wilderness of Topslip Tiger Forest, a hidden gem within the Anamalai Tiger Reserve. Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, Topslip is a haven for biodiversity enthusiasts, offering glimpses of majestic Bengal tigers, playful elephants, and a myriad of exotic flora and fauna. Explore the pristine beauty of this conservation paradise through guided safaris, nature walks, and trekking trails. Topslip beckons, promising a harmonious blend of adventure and tranquility in the heart of nature's embrace.

Airport : 1 hr 19 min (55km)

Railway station: 1 hr 5 min (41 km)

Anamalai Tiger Reserve

Anamalai Tiger Reserve, earlier known as Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary and National Park and as Anamalai Wildlife Sanctuary, is a protected area in the Anaimalai Hills of Pollachi and Valparai taluks of Coimbatore District and Udumalaipettai taluk in Tiruppur District, Tamil Nadu, India.

Airport : 3 hr 47 min (126.2km)

Railway station: 3 hr 27 min (111.6 km)
