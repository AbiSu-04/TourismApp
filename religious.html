Marudhamalai Arulmigu Subramanya Swami Temple

The Subramaniya Swamy Temple, Marudhamalai, or the Marudhamalai Murugan Temple, is a popular 12th-century hill temple situated in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.

Address: Maruthamalai Sri Subrahmanya Swamy Temple Rd, Marudhamalai Adivaram, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641046.

Airport : 1 hr 14 min (25km)

Railway Station: 44min (16km)

Perur Pateeshwarar Temple

Perur Pateeswarar Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva located at Perur, in western part of Coimbatore in state of Tamil Nadu in India. The temple was built by Karikala Chola in 2nd century CE.

Address: Siruvani Main Rd, Perur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641010.

Airport : 55 min (19km)

Railway station: 23min (7.3km)

Isha Yoga Temple

The foothills of the Velliangiri mountains, forty kilometers from the city of Coimbatore in South India, serve as home for the Isha Yoga Center. The Center is the residence for an active international community of Brahmacharis, full-time volunteers and visitors.

Address: 20, 9th St, Tatabad, Gandhipuram, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641012

Airport : 1 hr 30 min (40km)

Railway station: 57 min (30km)

Our Lady of Fatima Church

This church is pretty old and was inaugurated in January 1955 at St.Mary’s Primary School by Fr. Peter Damian. The church was blessed on December 23, 1962. It’s a beautiful church located near Gandhi Puram in Coimbatore. This is one of the best Catholic churches in Coimbatore.

Address: Dr Radhakrishna St, Doctor's Colony, Gandhipuram, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641012

Airport : 34 min (9.5km)

Railway station: 13 min (3.7km)

Athar Jamad Mosque

Athar Jamad Mosque is one of the oldest and biggest mosques in Coimbatore that is located at Oppanakara Street of Coimbatore district.The construction of this strong structure was started in 1860 with the use of lime and mortar and successfully completed in the year of 1904.

Address: Oppanakara St., Prakasam, Ukkadam, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu 641001

Airport: 42 min (11.7km)

Railway station: 5 min (1km)
